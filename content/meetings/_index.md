We meet throughout the year — it’s one of the best parts of being in the club. A chance to catch up with fellow enthusiasts, hear what everyone’s been up to, and generally have a good time. Why not come along for a chat, with or without your classic. We will be glad to see you, whatever you drive (or not).

## When We Meet

The Club meets on the third Tuesday, most months, from 7:00 pm - Meetings start at 7:30pm.
And we meet at other times too. Check our [calendar](/calendar/) for the current schedule and any updates.

## Venue

Our Club meetings are held at the Philanthropic Inn, 22 Twyncarn Road, Pontywaun, Cwmcarn, Gwent NP11 7DU. www.facebook.com/philanthropicinnpontywaun/

There is a car park at the rear - via small lane immediately adjacent on right hand side of Inn from road. Then access to car park on left hand side at end of lane (55 metres). Or park on the road, being considerate of neighbours.
A big thank you to the Philanthropic Inn for hosting Gwent MG Owners Club.

## What to Expect

Meetings are relaxed and informal. Following a formal sharing of club information, we usually have a quiz and a raffle, and we occasionally have speakers on a range of topics. It’s a great opportunity for members to chat, have a laugh, and get advice in a cosy pub setting. Come as you are. There’s usually a chance to:

  •	Catch up over a drink
  
  •	Talk about your car (and everyone else’s)
  
  •	Hear about upcoming events and runs
  
  •	Meet new members and old friends

If you’re thinking about joining, a meeting is the perfect way to come and see what we’re about. Just turn up — you’ll be made to feel very welcome.

Or, come and see us at one of the shows – see our calendar 
Want to know more? Just get in touch.
